Humba is a Filipino dish made from pork belly cooked slowly in soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and spices. It’s sweet, savory, and very tender.

Benefits:
•Good Source of Protein.
-Pork provides protein, which helps build and repair muscles and supports body growth.
•Provides Vitamins and Minerals.
-Humba contains:
Iron – for healthy blood
Zinc – boosts immune system
B vitamins (B1, B6, B12) – good for energy and brain function
•Contains Healthy Compounds from Spices.
-Garlic, vinegar, and bay leaves have antioxidants that may support digestion and heart health.

Advantages:
-Energy-rich because of its fat content.
-Easy to cook and perfect for family meals.
-Lasts longer due to vinegar and soy sauce.

- Calories: 350–500
-Protein: 15–20 g
-Fat: 25–35 g
-Carbohydrates 10–18 g
-Sodium High (because of soy sauce)
-Iron 1–2 mg
-Vitamin B6 0.2–0.4 mg
