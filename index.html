History

Tinny’s Store began with a simple dream in 2018—a plan to build a small business that
would help support the family and serve the people in the community.
Through hard work, determination,
and constant prayer, this dream slowly turned into reality.

The store was founded by Hilda Gumera,
whose dedication and effort became the heart of the business.
With limited resources but strong faith, she started Tinny’s Store
and managed it with patience and persistence.
Over the years, the store grew little by little,
becoming a trusted place where residents could buy everyday goods.

From 2018 up to now, Tinny’s Store
continues to operate and serve the community. Its growth
stands as a reminder that with effort and prayer,
even small beginnings can lead to lasting success.
As the owner of Tinny’s Store, Hilda Gumera faced countless challenges from the very beginning. Starting with limited resources, she had to balance her time between managing the store, caring for her family, and handling daily responsibilities. Competition from other stores made it difficult to keep a steady income, and there were times when sales went up and down. Despite these struggles, Hilda continued to work patiently, waking up early, praying for strength, and pushing forward even during tough days. Her journey shows that behind every small business is a story of sacrifice, resilience, and a strong will to provide a better future for her family.

THE SUCCESS STORY

Tinny’s Store, founded by Hilda Gumera in 2018, began as a simple but determined effort to provide for her family.

Through years of hard work, long hours, and steady faith, Hilda managed to keep the business strong despite competition and the challenges of daily life.

Feature the Owner
‎

Hilda Gumera, founder of Tinny’s Store, is a hardworking mother whose determination and faith turned a simple dream into a thriving community business.

Born and raised in Boljoon, Cebu, Hilda faced many challenges, but her perseverance and love for her children became her greatest inspiration.

Through paningkamot and pag-ampo, she established Tinny’s Store in 2018, starting from small resources and growing it with patience and dedication.

Today, the store continues to serve the community, reflecting Hilda’s commitment, resilience, and hope for a better future for her family.

MESSAGE FROM THE OWNER

The Government of the Philippines Helped the Business Grow

The government of the Philippines helped the growth of Tinny’s Store by providing support for small businesses through local government units. Programs such as business registration assistance, barangay and municipal permits, and small-business seminars helped the owner understand proper business management. The local government also supports small entrepreneurs by maintaining peace and order in the community, which helps businesses operate safely. These forms of support made it easier for Tinny’s Store to continue operating legally and steadily over the years.
